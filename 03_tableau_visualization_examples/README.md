# Tableau Visualization Examples

Part of the [AI Business Portfolio](../README.md).

Selected Tableau visualization examples focused on visual storytelling, chart selection, geospatial analysis, deviation analysis, and translating public data into business-facing questions.

This folder includes screenshots only. It does not include Tableau workbook files, professor-provided slides, assignment instructions, solution materials, private data, or restricted course materials.

## Included Visualizations

- `screenshots/flight_delays_phx_southwest.png` — flight route map showing Southwest Airlines departures from Phoenix Sky Harbor, color-encoded by average departure delay and sized by flight volume.
- `screenshots/housing_affordability_income_gap_by_city.png` — Gantt-style deviation chart comparing median household income against estimated minimum income requirements by city, with affordable and unaffordable thresholds highlighted.
- `screenshots/nba_player_shot_chart.png` — Tableau shot chart showing field-goal attempt locations, shot volume, and shooting efficiency on a custom basketball-court background.

## Analytic Questions

The examples address three different visualization problems:

- Which airline has the best departure performance out of Phoenix Sky Harbor?
- Which cities have the largest housing affordability gaps?
- How can player shot selection and shooting efficiency be visualized spatially?

## Methods & Tools

- Tableau Desktop, Tableau Public
- Tableau storybook navigation
- Geospatial route mapping
- Gantt-style deviation analysis
- Custom background-image spatial visualization
- Dual encoding for multivariate comparison

## Data Sources

The examples use public or course-provided public datasets, including U.S. Department of Transportation on-time performance data, housing affordability data, and public NBA shot-location data.
